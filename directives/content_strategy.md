## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Targets
**Primary**: AI automation, workflow automation, business automation
**Secondary**: AI training, automation consulting, custom automation development
**Long-tail**: AI automation for [industry], workflow optimization tools, AI agent development

### Technical SEO
- Unique title and meta description per page
- H1 hierarchy (one per page)
- Structured data (Organization, LocalBusiness if applicable)
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Image alt text
- Internal linking between service pages

### Content SEO
- Clear headings (H2, H3) for scanability
- Bulleted lists for key points
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Natural keyword integration (no stuffing)
- Action-oriented CTAs
